When prompted type R for "Rock", P for "Paper" or S for "Scissors"
Once Computer chooses its option, the options will be compared.
If options chosen by computer matches your its a tie. If you choose R and computer chooses S. You win.
If you choose P and computer chooses R. You win.
If you choose S and computer chooses P. You win.
Else you loose
When prompted type Y to continue playing else type N to end the game
Happy Playing
